Baked Blueberry Pudding

Makes 9 servings.

2 cups flour 1 cup sugar 2 teaspoons baking powder3/4 teaspoon nutmeg 1/2 teaspoon salt1/ teaspoon grated lemon peel3/4 cup butter2 eggs, beaten3/4 cup milk2 cups blueberriesLemon Sauce
Mix together flour sugar baking powdernutmegsalt and lemon peel. cut in butter with a pastry blender untill size of small peas. Add eggs and milk at low speed of mixer 3 minutes or until thoroughly blended. pour into greased and floured 9x9x2 inch pan and top with blueberries. Bake at 350 degrees 1 hour 10 minutes or unlitl tester comes out cleam. Serve warm with lemon Sauce.
